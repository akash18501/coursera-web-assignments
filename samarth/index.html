Shell Cores

A core is a device used in casting processes to produce internal cavities and reentrant angles. They are most commonly used in sand casting, but are also used in die casting and injection moulding......

Learn more

Shell Moulds

Supporting both ferrous and non-ferrous metals, shell molding is a molding process that’s characterized by the use of a sand-base mold. Companies don’t use just regular “beach” sand for this process.....

Learn more

Non-Ferrous Castings

Non-ferrous alloys do not contain iron in appreciable amounts. Common materials are aluminum, brass, copper and titanium....
