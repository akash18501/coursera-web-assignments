ABOUT SAMARTH INDUSTRIES

We are manufacturers and suppliers of qualitative range of Shell Cores, Shell Moulds and Non-Ferrous Castings to our clients for their foundry, production and installation needs.

We established in 2001 and since then we are highly acknowledged by our clients for our products quality, functional life, supreme efficiency, high performance and resistant to corrosion.

We can provide high quality of customized  products of various sizes. With our extensive experience in making cores , moulds and non ferrous castings, we can manufacture the products with higher accuracy . We keep on investing in technology advancement to keep pace with expectations of our customer.

With our expertise and consultancy, we support our clients to improve their bottom lines by providing quality products with enhanced product life and better manufacturing accuracy thus reducing the operations cost or maintenance cost. While working with our customers, we understand the requirements in details along with product expectation. Our final objective is to ensure the customer satisfaction.

We always try to deliver the product within defined timelines thus our team work closely work with our customers since from concept inception, to its design and finally its execution, to avoid any last minute product quality surprises.

We always believing in empowering our people through suitable technological trainings. Samarth Industries firmly believes that Product is an outcome of Technology and People. So advancement for both pillars should go hand in hand.

We are a Start-up India initiative, having a team of experts committed to provide tailor made products and its implementation across the industries. We believe that improvement in product quality is a continuous process and can be achieved by technology driven innovations.
